Step 4

Step 5

API Key Generated

Your API key is now generated!

Step 6

üì± Install App on iPhone/iPad

Add the Bhagavad Gita app to your home screen for a native app experience - works offline and launches instantly!

Step 1

Open in Safari

Make sure you're viewing the app in Safari browser. Tap the "three dot" button at the bottom of the screen.

Step 2

Tap Share button

Tap the "Share" button on screen.

Step 3

Tap More button

Tap the "More" button on screen.

Step 4

Find Add to Home Screen

Scroll down in the share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen" option.

Confirm App Name

You can customize the app name or keep "Bhagavad Gita". Tap "Add" in the top right corner.
